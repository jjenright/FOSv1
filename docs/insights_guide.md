# Financial Insights Guide

## Purpose

The v0.6.0 insight layer converts validated FOS records into a small number of
repeatable observations and measurable actions. It does not use generative AI,
and the same inputs always produce the same outputs.

## Workbook sheets

### Insights

Six ranked observations covering liquidity, unsecured debt, fixed-cost pressure,
wealth building, spending movement, and data quality. Each observation includes
evidence, the implication, and a recommended action.

### Action_Plan

Six measurable actions with current values, targets, gaps or headroom, the source
measurement, and a status indicator.

### Spending_Evolution

Compares the latest complete year with the prior comparison year and the average
of up to three preceding comparison-eligible years. Partial years are excluded
from direct comparisons.

### Insight_Definitions

Documents every operating rule and its limitation.

## Internal FOS operating rules

- Recent benchmark: up to three preceding comparison-eligible years.
- Liquidity target: three months of latest core spending.
- Fixed-cost watch level: 30% of latest true income.
- Wealth-building floor: 10% of latest true income.
- Spending focus: largest mapped dollar increases versus the prior year.

These thresholds are directional FOS rules, not industry standards or individualized
investment recommendations.
