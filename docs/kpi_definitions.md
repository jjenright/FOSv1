# KPI Definitions

The workbook contains the authoritative `KPI_Definitions` sheet. Key definitions:

- **True Income:** INC001 + INC002 + INC003; excludes cash-flow adjustment rows.
- **Known Operating Expenses:** mapped fixed, variable, irregular, and capital outflows.
- **Wealth-Building Rate:** savings, TFSA, RRSP/pension, and RESP transfers divided by true income.
- **Savings Velocity:** wealth building plus targeted LOC/student-loan reduction divided by true income.
- **Financial Flexibility:** income remaining after fixed expenses and targeted debt reduction, before variable living costs.
- **Comparison Eligible:** complete year with at least 85% mapped outflow coverage.
- **FPI v1:** provisional household-specific pressure score; not an industry-standard measure.
